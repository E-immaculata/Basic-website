My Mission

My mission at ALCHE is to use technology and innovation to create opportunities for young people, especially girls who have been denied access to education or forced into early marriage and child labor. Through software engineering, I aim to tackle the Global Challenge of Job Creation and embrace the Global Opportunity of Youth Empowerment. I am building solutions that open doors to learning, employment, and self-reliance turning pain into purpose and purpose into impact.

The impact of my mission is about changing lives. Through technology and innovation, I empower girls who have been denied education or forced into early marriage and child labor to learn, grow, and take control of their futures. They gain skills, confidence, and independence, opening doors to education, careers, and new opportunities. Communities benefit as these girls become role models, inspiring others and shifting mindsets toward equality and opportunity. Partnering with NGO's to fully bring to life my project called DeRemedy Foundation, my mission ensures that vulnerable children receive care, education, and guidance while also gaining practical skills to thrive. Together, we turn barriers into opportunities and challenges into real, lasting impact.
